# Project: HR Management (AI-First HR Tool)

## 🎯 Vision
A cost-disruptive, AI-powered HR management tool that automates employee lifecycle tasks, data management, and self-service. By leveraging AI for building and maintaining the platform, we can offer premium HR features at a fraction of the cost of legacy SaaS incumbents.

## 🛠️ Core Features (Initial Idea)
- **Employee Directory:** Centralized management of employee profiles and data.
- **Self-Service Portal:** Employees can manage their own details, requests, and documents.
- **Automated Workflows:** AI-triggered reminders and emails (e.g., vacation auto-replies, holiday notifications, compliance checks).
- **Cost Advantage:** Significant price disruption compared to existing HR suites.

## 📍 Infrastructure
